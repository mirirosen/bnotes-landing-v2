## Product Overview

**One-liner:**
תוסף Chrome שהופך הרצאות אונליין לסיכום לימודי ברור ומסודר — עם תמלול בזמן אמת.

**What it does:**
B Notes is a Chrome extension for online lectures. Transcription appears in real time during the lecture. At the end of recording, a structured AI summary is produced — organized by topics and ready for study.

**Product category:**
כלי לימוד להרצאות מקוונות / תוסף Chrome לתמלול וסיכום הרצאות.

**Product type:**
Chrome extension (browser add-on).

**Official URLs:**
- Production site: https://bnotes.ai/
- Chrome Web Store: https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/b-notes/djdmbmibfjjmgoacidjlnholckcaplli
- Privacy policy: https://bnotes.ai/privacy

**Brand name rule:**
Use **B Notes** everywhere. Do not use the old name **Lectify**.

**Business model (verified):**
- 7-day free trial
- ₪9.90 per month afterward
- Cancel anytime

---

## Target Audience

**Target users:**
Israeli students who study through online lectures.

**Primary use case:**
Watch an online lecture and end up with structured study material — not just raw transcription.

**Jobs to be done:**
1. Follow the lecture without splitting attention on manual note-taking
2. Get real-time transcription as the lecture progresses
3. Receive a structured summary at the end, ready for study and export

## Problems & Pain Points

**Core problem:**
Online lectures deliver dense continuous information. Students struggle to capture and organize it into usable study material.

**Emotional tension (hypothesis — needs customer quotes):**
"אני שומע/ת הכל אבל לא יוצא לי מסודר", "קשה לרשום ולהקשיב ביחד".

---

## Differentiation (verified framing)

- Works during the lecture (real-time transcription)
- Output is **structured** study summary — not raw transcript
- Works across major lecture platforms in the browser
- Hebrew and English support
- Export to Word, PDF, NotebookLM

**Do not claim without evidence:**
- User counts ("thousands of students")
- Accuracy statistics
- Time-saving quantification
- University logos or endorsements
