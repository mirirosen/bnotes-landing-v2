*Phase 1 — strategy*

## FINAL DECISION (Phase 2 approval — supersedes the Phase 1 recommendation below)

**Approved:** Direction A ("המחברת הנבנית") as the primary brand direction, combined with the structural restraint and diagram logic of Direction B.

**Not selected:** Direction C ("בתוך ההרצאה") is not the primary direction. Its literal browser/lecture framing still informs the product-demo and how-it-works sections for five-second clarity, but the dominant visual and typographic language is A+B.

**Refined core transformation (approved):**
עומס בהרצאה אונליין → תמלול בזמן אמת → מיון וארגון לנושאים ולמדרג → סיכום לימודי מסודר.

**Signature element (approved, refined from Direction A):**
One continuous hierarchy line gathers scattered Hebrew lecture fragments into headings, subheadings, and bullet points — used prominently once in the hero, with restrained echoes elsewhere (not repeated as decoration in every section).

The Phase 1 comparison and critique below is preserved for reference; the recommendation section at the bottom reflects the original (superseded) proposal.

## Central Brand Transformation

All directions express the same product truth:

```
עומס קוגניטיבי מהרצאה אונליין
        ↓
  לכידת המידע הרלוונטי
        ↓
  ארגון לנושאים, כותרות וקשרים
        ↓
  סיכום לימודי ברור
```

The winning direction must make this legible within **five seconds** without looking like a generic AI SaaS template.

## Direction A — "המחברת הנבנית" (The Constructed Notebook)

### Strategic idea
Position B Notes as a **study craftsmanship tool**: the lecture is raw material; the output is a meticulously structured notebook page. The brand behaves like premium academic publishing meeting digital utility — not like a startup selling "AI magic."

### Emotional promise
"בסוף ההרצאה, יש לי מחברת."

Relief, order, dignity in studying. The student feels respected — not sold to.

### Visual world
- **Material metaphor:** High-quality study paper, margin notes, indentation hierarchy, subtle ruling lines.
- **Environment:** Abstracted desk surface — not a dorm cliché. Quiet morning study light.
- **Product depiction:** Real extension output rendered as beautiful typographic structure (once assets exist).
- **Signature element:** A single **live hierarchy line** — one continuous vertical structure line that "gathers" scattered lecture phrases into nested headings. Used once in hero, echoed subtly in section dividers.

### Typography direction
| Role | Direction |
|------|-----------|
| Display | **Fedra Sans HL** or **Narkiss Block** — confident Hebrew with editorial presence |
| Body | **Heebo** or **Assistant** — highly readable at small sizes, RTL-native |
| Structure | **IBM Plex Sans Hebrew** (medium) for headings/outline labels — signals organization |

Scale: Large, calm hero headline (2–3 lines max). Outline samples shown in real Hebrew sizes, not lorem.

### Color logic
| Name | Hex | Role |
|------|-----|------|
| Paper Ground | `#F6F3EC` | Primary background — warm but not cream-template default |
| Ink | `#1A1A1A` | Primary text |
| Margin Rule | `#C8C0B4` | Structural lines, dividers |
| Study Blue | `#2B4C7E` | Primary CTA, active states — academic, not neon |
| Soft Mark | `#E8DFD0` | Highlight bands behind outline examples |

**Avoid:** Purple gradients, glowing cards, dark mode with acid accent.

### Hero concept
Split composition (RTL: primary content on right):
- **Right:** Headline — "ההרצאה מסתיימת. הסיכום כבר מסודר."
- **Left:** Animated-but-restrained transformation panel: chaotic Hebrew lines (muted) collapse into a single indented outline block.
- **CTA:** "התקינו את B Notes ב-Chrome" (links to verified store URL only)
- **Chrome install badge:** Minimal, official-style — not a fake rating.

### Scroll World concept
**Architecture A — continuous forward take** through a physical-digital hybrid:
1. Overhead glide across a desk with an open laptop (lecture in progress, visual noise)
2. Forward into the browser depth — UI abstracted, not literal Google branding
3. Through the extension surface — information streams inward
4. Into a notebook page where lines become headings
5. Final settle on the completed summary + CTA card on desk

**Art direction:** Soft matte paper-diorama / architectural model — *not* clay toy default. Feels like a premium paper craft explainer.

### Strengths
- Strong premium / branding-agency appeal
- Typography does the selling — credible for education
- Outline structure is instantly readable as product value
- Degrades well to static layouts on mobile

### Risks
- Could feel slow or "editorial" if motion is overdone
- Paper metaphor may hide that this is a **browser extension** if hero is too abstract
- Requires exceptional Hebrew typographic craft — generic execution = boring

### Mobile behavior
- No scroll-scrub video required
- Hero transformation becomes stepped scroll reveal (3 states: noise → partial → outline)
- Sticky bottom CTA bar
- Large tap target for Chrome install

### Conversion implications
- **High clarity** if outline example is visible above fold
- **Trust-forward** — good for students skeptical of AI hype
- Needs real product screenshot early or risk feeling conceptual

### Critique scorecard

| Criterion | Assessment |
|-----------|------------|
| Originality | High — editorial academic is rare in Chrome extension landings |
| Brand relevance | High — maps directly to "structured study summary" |
| 5-second clarity | Medium-High — requires strong visual before/after |
| Credibility | High — if real output shown; Low if only abstract |
| Accessibility | High — type-led, can respect reduced motion easily |
| Mobile performance | High — mostly static |
| Technical feasibility | Medium — Scroll World assets needed; fallback viable |

## Direction B — "מבט מבנה" (Structural View)

### Strategic idea
Treat the lecture as **signal with noise**. B Notes is the instrument that reveals structure. Minimal, almost scientific — confidence through reduction, not decoration.

### Emotional promise
"אני רואה את המבנה."

Control, lucidity, intelligence without shouting about AI.

### Visual world
- Clean laboratory-for-thought aesthetic
- Sparse diagrams: topic nodes, grouping brackets, indentation trees
- Lecture represented as waveform + fragmented captions (abstracted, not literal transcript dump)
- No cards grid. No glassmorphism.

### Typography direction
| Role | Direction |
|------|-----------|
| Display | **Arimo** or **Rubik** (tight tracking, bold) — modern Israeli institutional |
| Body | **Assistant** — neutral, efficient |
| Mono | **JetBrains Mono** for outline hierarchy samples only |

### Color logic
| Name | Hex | Role |
|------|-----|------|
| Calm White | `#FAFAF8` | Background |
| Graphite | `#2E2E2E` | Text |
| Structure Green | `#3E6B5A` | Accent — growth/clarity, not neon |
| Fog | `#E6E6E2` | Diagram backgrounds |
| Signal | `#8A9BA8` | De-emphasized noise elements |

### Hero concept
Full-width typographic hero:
- Headline: "מהרצאה אונליין לסיכום לימודי — בזמן שאתם לומדים."
- Below: horizontal **structure strip** — 4 labeled states (not numbered 01/02): עומס → לכידה → מבנה → סיכום
- Center visual: animated grouping of topic labels into a tree (CSS/SVG, not video)
- CTA immediately visible

### Scroll World concept
**Architecture A** through abstract typographic space:
- Camera glides forward through floating Hebrew phrases in shallow depth
- Phrases cluster into labeled groups with bracket connectors
- Groups fold into document outline
- Final forward move to installation prompt as a clean panel

**Art direction:** Monochrome architectural model with single accent color — closer to museum exhibit than SaaS.

### Strengths
- Extremely distinct from AI landing templates
- Excellent accessibility and performance potential
- Structure metaphor is ownable
- Senior designers often respect restraint

### Risks
- **5-second clarity risk** — may feel conceptual before product context is clear
- Could read as "design studio concept" vs. "Chrome extension I need now"
- Cold tone might under-serve emotional exam-stress relief

### Mobile behavior
- SVG structure animation simplified to 2-frame crossfade
- Structure strip becomes vertical progression (labels, not numbers)
- CTA persistent

### Conversion implications
- Strong for educated audience; weaker if user needs immediate "what is this product physically?"
- Must add explicit "תוסף Chrome" label in hero eyebrow

### Critique scorecard

| Criterion | Assessment |
|-----------|------------|
| Originality | Very high |
| Brand relevance | Medium-High — structure yes, lecture context weaker |
| 5-second clarity | Medium — needs explicit product category label |
| Credibility | Medium — risks appearing pre-product |
| Accessibility | Very high |
| Mobile performance | Very high |
| Technical feasibility | High — lighter Scroll World + strong CSS fallback |

## Direction C — "בתוך ההרצאה" (Inside the Lecture)

### Strategic idea
Show the product **where it actually lives** — inside the browser, during the lecture. The brand is contextual, empathetic, literal: "we meet you in the lecture, not after."

### Emotional promise
"אני לא מפסיק ללמוד כדי לרשום — יוצא לי סיכום מסודר."

Recognition, flow, practical relief.

### Visual world
- Stylized browser viewport (generic chrome — no Google trademark misuse)
- Lecture player area with subtle motion (speaker, slides abstracted)
- B Notes extension panel as a designed UI object — real screenshots when available
- Study environment: Israeli student desk, headphones, notebook — human but not stock-photo cheesy

### Typography direction
| Role | Direction |
|------|-----------|
| Display | **Ploni** (or **Almoni Tzar**) — contemporary Israeli, approachable |
| Body | **Open Sans Hebrew** or **Assistant** |
| UI samples | System-like neutral sans for extension mockups |

### Color logic
| Name | Hex | Role |
|------|-----|------|
| Browser Ground | `#F0F2F5` | Page background |
| Lecture Dim | `#1E2430` | Lecture frame (contained, not full-page dark) |
| Panel Light | `#FFFFFF` | Extension panel |
| Action Coral | `#D4654A` | CTA — warm, human (single accent) |
| Link Blue | `#3A5F8C` | Secondary links |

**Note:** Dark is **contained to the lecture frame**, not a full dark-mode landing.

### Hero concept
- Eyebrow: "תוסף Chrome להרצאות אונליין"
- Headline: "ההרצאה רצה. הסיכום מתבנה לבד."
- Visual: Browser frame with lecture playing + extension panel showing outline forming
- Subhead: one sentence on structured study summary
- Primary CTA + "מה זה עושה בפועל?" scroll cue

### Scroll World concept
**Architecture A — continuous forward take** (recommended over diorama for this direction):

| Scene | Beat |
|-------|------|
| 1 | Outside: student browser with lecture starting — sensory overload (tabs, timer, slides, speech captions) |
| 2 | Forward through browser into lecture viewport |
| 3 | Extension panel enters; information organizes into topics |
| 4 | Summary document resolves — clear headings and relationships |
| 5 | Pull back slightly to student finishing lecture, CTA to install |

**Improvement over default storyboard:** Scene 1 should establish **lecture-in-progress stress**, not a static room. Scene 3 should show **structure appearing during lecture**, not post-processing. Scene 5 should be **outcome-first** (study material ready), not a celebration cliché.

**Architecture B rejected for this product:** Dive-out connectors read as "rewind" — wrong for a flow-state study tool.

### Strengths
- **Highest 5-second clarity** — user sees browser + lecture + outcome
- Best Scroll World narrative fidelity to actual product context
- Strongest conversion path for Chrome extension
- Emotional recognition for target audience

### Risks
- Can look like a feature demo if art direction is weak
- UI-heavy visuals age quickly if product UI changes
- Scroll World production cost and mobile beta limitations
- Literal approach may feel less "award-winning" than Direction A/B if not elevated typographically

### Mobile behavior
- Scroll World: desktop-only video; mobile gets **5 key stills** from scenes + swipe progression
- Hero: static composite screenshot/poster
- Sticky install CTA
- Reduced motion: step-through with stills

### Conversion implications
- Best CTA proximity to demonstrated value
- Easiest to answer "what is this?" immediately
- Must avoid looking like every screen-recording SaaS demo — elevate with typography and pacing

### Critique scorecard

| Criterion | Assessment |
|-----------|------------|
| Originality | Medium — literal product landings are common; elevation needed |
| Brand relevance | Very high |
| 5-second clarity | Very high |
| Credibility | High with real UI; Medium with mock UI |
| Accessibility | Medium — video-heavy without fallback |
| Mobile performance | Medium — needs disciplined fallback |
| Technical feasibility | Medium — Scroll World + ffmpeg + Higgsfield credits |

## Recommendation

**Primary direction: C — "בתוך ההרצאה"**
**With typographic craft borrowed from A — "המחברת הנבנית"**

### Rationale

1. **Five-second clarity is non-negotiable** for a Chrome extension CTA. Direction C shows the product context immediately.
2. **Scroll World storyboard maps to real user journey** — the recommended 5-scene arc is strong if Scene 1 emphasizes overload and Scene 3 shows in-lecture structuring (see `scroll-world-storyboard.md`).
3. **Conversion goal favors recognition over abstraction.** Students should feel "this is for my lecture workflow."
4. **Premium elevation comes from execution, not abstraction:** Use Direction A's editorial outline typography for the summary reveal (Scene 4–5) and section typography across the page. Avoid demo-landing cheapness.
5. **Direction B is a strong secondary layer** for the "how it works" section — structure strip and diagram language can appear mid-page without defining the whole brand.

### What we are NOT recommending
- Full dark/neon aesthetic
- Decorative 01/02/03 section numbering
- Generic glowing feature cards
- Diorama pull-out Scroll World connectors (Architecture B)
